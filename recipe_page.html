Effortless Recipes

Here is a list of our most popular dishes for any kitchen.

Pasta with Cherry Tomato Sauce

  1. Boil Water and add a pinch of salt
  2. Add pasta and follow cooking directions
  3. Heat Garlic and 60ml of olive oil in a skillet for 3 minutes
  4. Cook until sauce is creamy
  5. When pasta is cooked, add sauce
  6. Serve with a dash of parmesan.

Butternut Squash Pasta

  1. Heat olive oil on very high heat for about 3 minutes before adding squash, salt and pepper

  2. Combine sauce and pasta into a pan with room temp water and bring to a boil for about 5 minutes

  3. Mix into a skillet with some pasta water and stir in a cheese of your choice

Mango Chicken with Vegetables

  1. Season Chicken with Pepper and Mango Chutney

  2. Place chicken onto a baking sheet with 1 tbsp. of olive oil and a pinch of salt

  3. Add vegtables to baking sheet

  4. Bake until cooked thoroughly

Fried Chicken and Rice

  1. Heat 1 tbsp. of oil in a wok or skillet.
  2. Add chicken, pepper, garlic and ginger and wait 2 minutes.
  3. Add broccoli and wait another 3 minutes.
  4. Add another 3 tbsp of oil and add rice along with a raw egg.

Carolina Style Sloppy Joe

  1. Preheat grill to 400 degrees.
  2. Mix mayonnaise, sour cream, apple cider vinegar, salt and pepper into a bowl.
  3. Combine ground sitloin and steak sauce and grill till cooked.
  4. Combine mix from bowl and serve.

Oven-Baked Salmon

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F.
  2. Season salmon with salt and pepper.
  3. Place salmon, skin side down in a non-stick pan or baking sheet.
  4. Cook for about 12 to 15 min, until fully cooked.
  5. Serve with delicious cashew salad.

Pot Roast

  1. Preheat oven to 275 degrees F.
  2. Add olive oil. Brown onions and carrots, set aside.
  3. Sear roast and set aside.
  4. Add red wine or beef broth along with roast.
  5. Add cooked onions, carrots, and herbs, and cook for 3 to 5 hours.

Chicken Enchiladas

  1. Season chicken and brown over medium heat. Set aside.
  2. Saute onion and garlic in drippings. Add canned tomatoes.
  3. Add shredded chicken and microwave tortillas for 30 sec.
  4. Fill tortilla with mixture and bake in 350 degrees F for 15 min.

Spinach Artichoke Dip

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Combine sour cream, pepper, garlic, artichoke hearts, spinach, cream mozzarella cheese.
  3. Top with mozzarella cheese and parmesan.
  4. Put in oven for 30 min or until bubbly.

Pumpkin Pie

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
  2. Combine sugar, salt, cinnamon, ginger, and cloves. Beat eggs.
  3. Stir in pumpkin, sugar-spice mixture, and evaporated milk.
  4. Pour into pie shell and bake for 15 min.
  5. Reduce heat to 350 degrees F and bake for 40 to 50 min.
